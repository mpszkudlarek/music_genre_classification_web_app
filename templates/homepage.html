Music Genre Recognition


Discover the genres of unknown songs with ease. App powered by deep learning and built with Flask. Enjoy learning about new music genres effortlessly!


How to Use the Music Genre Recognition App?

  1. Load the song whose genre you want to recognize.
  2. Ensure the file is in WAV or MP3 format and is at least 30 seconds long to get an accurate prediction.
  3. Click the 'Recognize' button.
